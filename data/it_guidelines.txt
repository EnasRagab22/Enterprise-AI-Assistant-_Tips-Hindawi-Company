
IT Guidelines:
1. Password must be changed every 90 days.
2. VPN must be used for remote access.
3. All software installations must be approved by IT department.
4. Company devices must have antivirus installed and updated.
5. Data backups should be done weekly and stored securely.
6. Multi-factor authentication must be enabled for all accounts.
7. Use encrypted storage for sensitive documents.
8. Do not share passwords with anyone.
9. IT tickets must be logged for any hardware issues.
10. Follow IT guidelines for company-provided mobile devices.
